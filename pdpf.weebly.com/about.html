About Pankaj:
     Pankaj Dhande is an enthusiastic hobby photographer based out of Pune, Maharashtra. Since the childhood, Pankaj has developed a serene relationship with the Nature. His love towards nature can be easily understood from his photographs. Pankaj specializes in landscape and travel photography. Born in Aurangabad, India, Pankaj wish to explore the un-noticed places in Maharashtra and aims to travel abroad for the photo-shoots. Though he likes sitting at home and enjoying a cup of tea watching television, he is always ready to leave the house for a photo-shoot. Though being an amateur  photographer, Pankaj has been constantly working towards improving his photography skills.

﻿Photography Gear: ﻿
     When it comes to photography gear, Pankaj is always asked about the gear he uses. Pankaj actually started with his cell phone camera, which was 2Mp camera of Nokia 5235.. Later he switched to Nikon Coolpix L120, which is a point and shoot camera, and later switched to a DSLR.
